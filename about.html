THE SCIENCE OF KAAVA

UAS Dharwad: The Bio-Fortification Frontier

Our partnership with the University of Agricultural Sciences, Dharwad focuses on developing naturally Iron-Rich and Zinc-Rich bio-fortified formulations. Unlike synthetic additives, these minerals are bonded within the plant's molecular structure, ensuring maximum absorption and zero digestive distress.

This breakthrough directly powers our core performance blends, targeting ATP production and oxygen transport in elite athletes. We are proud to announce that our collaborative bio-fortified formulation was recently showcased at AAHAR 2026 in Delhi by the esteemed UAS Dharwad research team , marking a major milestone in Indian agricultural and sports science.

Kodaikanal Station: High-Altitude Adaptogens

High above the cloud line, we collaborate with the Horticulture Research Station in Kodaikanal to study indigenous flora that survives in extreme environments. These high-altitude adaptogens, including specialized strains of Ashwagandha and Brahmi, possess unique phytochemical profiles that help the human body manage cortisol levels and accelerate cellular recovery after grueling endurance sessions.

A Bio Sciences University: Fermentation Elixirs

Nutrition is not just about what you eat, but how your body sustains it. Our joint clinical trials with a Bio Sciences University in Bangalore have pioneered advanced fermentation techniques using sweet potato elixirs. By leveraging this specialized fermentation process, we have developed a unique complex carbohydrate matrix that delivers higher stamina and sustained energy across our product range, ensuring that every drop of Kaava translates into pure endurance.
